This Privacy Policy outlines Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd’s policy and responsibility in relation to the collection, use and disclosure of your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 26 of 2012) and any subsidiary legislation (the “Act”).

Your participation in such activities is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice in submitting your personal data.

1. Types of Personal Data Collected


The types of personal data that Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd collects depend on the purposes/occasions for the collection or the nature of the activity/transaction involved. We may collect the following types of data which may amount to personal data:

- Name, NRIC or other identification number, date of birth, address, contact number, CCTV footage, photographs, email address or IP address;

An individual’s spending or redemption data such as date of purchases, purchase amount, shop name, date of redemption and redemption item(s); or

Demographic data such as gender, age, area of residence, monthly income.

If you provide personal data relating to a third party (e.g. information about your dependent, spouse, children and/or parents) to us, you represent and warrant that the consent of that third party has been given/obtained for our collection, use and disclosure of their personal data in connection with the purposes specified below and that such personal data is accurate and complete.

2. Purposes for Collection, Use and Disclosure


Generally, Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may collect, use and/or disclose personal data for the following purposes:

To facilitate redemption activities. For instance, if you wish to redeem an item from us at any Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd branches, your personal data is collected and/or used to verify your identification and process your redemption.

To facilitate lucky draws or other contests or activities conducted by Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd. For instance, if you participate in a lucky draw, your personal data may also be used to contact you or to notify you subsequently if you win a prize.

To improve our services. For instance, if you participate in our surveys, we may use your feedback/suggestions to improve the shopping experience at Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd.

To inform you of Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd’s promotions and updates. For instance, if you subscribe to Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd’s mailing list, we may communicate with you via e-mail updates in relation to special offers or activities offered by us.

To process and respond to your enquiries, feedback, requests, complaints or incident reports. For instance, if you send feedback to Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd, we may use your contact information to communicate with you.

To generate internal reports such as annual, operational or management reports.

For security purposes. For instance, if you enter Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd’s premises, surveillance cameras may capture your images. You photographs may also be captured if you attend events or activities held on Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd’s premises.

To resolve any disputes which may arise between Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd and you.

For any investigation or proceedings where your personal data is relevant. For instance, if you wish to report to us an incident at Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd’s premises, we may collect your particulars to respond to you or conduct further investigations.

To process and administer any insurance claims or payments arising under or in connection with incidents.

To comply with applicable laws.

For evaluative purposes as defined under the Act;

For other specific purposes for which your personal data was provided.

Disclosure to Third Parties


Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd will use reasonable efforts to ensure that its employees and other third parties who are involved in the collection, use and disclosure of personal data will observe and adhere to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

We will not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your personal data with our related entities or other third parties in connection with the purposes contemplated by this Privacy Policy.

Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may disclose your personal data to law enforcement or government agencies for security/legal purposes. Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd also has the right to share your personal data as is necessary to prevent a threat to the life, health or security of an individual. Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may disclose personal data, as is necessary, to investigate suspected unlawful activities including but not limited to fraud and intellectual property rights infringement.

3. Withdrawal



Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd gives you the option of opting out of receiving newsletters and marketing materials from us. If you do not wish to receive any marketing materials from us, you can indicate your preference at the office of Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd.

For newsletters and marketing materials communicated to you via email, you may opt-out of receiving them by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link in the latest email containing such newsletter or material or by sending an email to admin@zionauto.sg to indicate that you wish to unsubscribe.

You may also withdraw your consent in relation to other purposes by writing to us. Where applicable, Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd will inform you of the likely consequences of such withdrawal. If you have given your consent for multiple purposes, please note that your personal data may still be collected, disclosed and used for purposes for which no withdrawal of consent was made. Please note that even upon complete withdrawal of consent, Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may still retain Personal Data necessary for legal and business reasons or as may be required by law.

Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may, upon your written request to us, allow you to access your personal data in our possession or control.

Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd has the right to reject frivolous or vexatious requests or requests for trivial information or any request on other grounds provided under the Act.

5. Retention



Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd will only retain your personal data for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws.

6. Accuracy



Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd needs your assistance to ensure that your personal data is current, complete and accurate. As such, please inform Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd of any changes or errors in your personal data by contacting Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd and submitting your updated particulars to Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd in writing. Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may also request from you updates on your personal data from time to time.

7. Protection and Verification



The security of your personal data is important to us. We will protect your personal data by adopting reasonable measures to reduce the risks of unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal or similar risks.

Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may adopt verification practices and processes to verify your identity or to process your requests. For example, when you redeem a gift with Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd, Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd may require your personal data such as your telephone number to call or interact with you.

Zion Auto Gallery Pte Ltd reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy from time to time without prior notice. Please check this Page at www.zionauto.sg for the latest and most current Privacy Policy.
