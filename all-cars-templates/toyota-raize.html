Eco Maximising

Toyota Raize is a compact Family SUV. Measuring under 4000mm in length, 1695mm in width, but have a class-leading 369L luggage capacity. If you fold the rear seats it will opening up a big cargo space for your shopping.

Effortless Driving

Raize has a 1.0-liter turbo engine, combined with a CVT transmission. Toyota says that Raize achieves the torque and driving joy of a car with a 1.5-liter engine.

Modern & Traditional

Another standard feature for the Raize is the Keyless Start/Stop Function, it offers you a seamless entry and engine start with just a push of a button, giving you a more convenient to get-in and go experience.
