Domineering Stance

If it’s road presence you need, then the Mercedes-Benz GLE450 has it in abundance.At nearly five metres long (4924mm to be exact), 1947mm wide and 1772mm tall, the GLE450 sticks out in any carpark.

Boundless Room Space

The GLE450’s cockpit feels massive. There’s plenty of headroom and shoulder room, and there’s a wide gulf between you and the front passenger.
