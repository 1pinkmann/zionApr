Redefining Mordern

Engines are rarely a defining feature of modern hot hatchbacks, with everything homogenised to the same four-cylinder turbocharged norm and differentiated largely by throttle maps.

Nothing Left Untouched

In terms of cabin design, though, the CLA45 S hasn’t left anything on the table. The twin 10.25-inch screens – one for infotainment, the other a configurable driver display – offer a commanding presence atop the two-tiered dash.

Sporty & Luxurious

It features all the bells and whistles as standard, too: satellite navigation, Apple and Android smartphone mirroring, DAB+ radio, and a trick 12-speaker Burmester sound system, all of which can be accessed in myriad ways.
