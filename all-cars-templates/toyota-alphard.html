Smooth Driving

Alphard feels gentle and cosseting when gathering speed. The transmission is smooth but responsive, and the engine doesn’t vibrate alarmingly even when you floor the throttle pedal.

Best Experience

The Alphard is best experienced from the back, specifically in Row Two of its sizeable cabin. There’s an enormous amount of room inside, so the middle row chairs are as plush as Osim’s finest efforts.

Enormouse Cargo Space

Mind you, Toyota says that even if every seat is occupied, you can still shove six golf bags into the boot. And there’s a multitude of storage bins around the cabin, and even pockets here and there.
